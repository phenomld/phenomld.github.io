SUMMER CAMP 2016

CAMP FORMAT

To ensure top-level instruction and competition, a set number of players for each position will be selected based on skill and experience. The first two days will be dedicated to specialized, position specific drills to enhance players' understanding of the nuances of each position. The final two days will comprise of group play with all positions to practice newly learned skills and insights. The last two days will also have Choice Sessions, where players can decide which skills they wish to improve, including but not limited to: face offs, agility/endurance training, and stick checking.

Please complete the application in order to be considered for this amazing experience in lacrosse education. After completion, the coaches will review applications and select players based on their experience, recommendations, and space. There are a limited number of spots for each position, and registration closes July 1st, so APPLY NOW!

Includes: camp fee, shooting shirt, and a reversible.

COACHING STAFF

DJ Shelton
COACH

Coach DJ Shelton is a Palo Alto native, and the head varsity coach of Palo Alto High School, where he won 2014 SCVAL Coach of the Year Award, and won the 2015 SCVAL Championship. He played at Goucher College (Div. III) in Baltimore, MD, where he played defense and was a face off specialist. Goucher made two NCAA tournament appearances during his time there, and was ranked #10 in the nation his senior year. Coach Shelton has 12 years of coaching experience of all ages, and founded Phenom in high school to start giving back to the game that had given him so much.

Bubba Paguirigan
COACH

Coach Manuel “Bubba” Paguirigan is currently an assistant coach for the boys lacrosse team at Palo Alto High School. Before Paly, Bubba coached at Santa Clara University, Sequoia High School, and at the youth level for the Firehawks and Tomahawks programs. Growing up in Palo Alto, he has been playing for 16 years and played alongside Coach Shelton on the Tomahawks and Redhawks. Since high school, Bubba has focused on giving back by sharing his love and passion for the game to all ages.

ABOUT US

The Phenom Mission

Phenom Lacrosse recruits small groups of players to ensure individualized coaching for a stronger understanding of lacrosse.

Our Philosophy

The objective is to turn the field into a classroom. Sessions are designed for the players to receive top-level instruction through specialized, position-specific drills, and then to utilize them in live play. In a competitive atmosphere, players will have the game broken down for them to fine-tune their fundamental skills and develop a stronger lacrosse IQ.
